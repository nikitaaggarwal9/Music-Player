About

Genius Annotation

Charlie Puth first shared “That’s Hilarious” on his TikTok on October 12, 2021 while in the studio, showing the production of the song.

On February 14, 2022 after finishing the song, Charlie shared in his TikTok,

When I listen to it, it brings me back to the hardest time in my life when I couldn’t let somebody go. It makes me sad, which I never am on here, but I hope you love it. Don’t give your heart to someone who’s still got a broken one.

On March 23, 2022 Charlie announced on TikTok “That’s Hilarious” being the second single off his third-studio album, Charlie. In his TikTok, Charlie expressed through tears,

I’m really excited for the song to come out but, you know, every time I hear it, I’m kind of brought back to a time that was really, really challenging in my life.

What have the artists said about the song?
Genius Answer

I almost shelved “That’s Hilarious” because I was afraid that the painful memories would come back up every time I’d sing it. But you all helped me realize that this is how you heal.

—via Twitter

What have the artists said about the song?
Genius Answer

All of these songs come with combining an ugly and a beautiful thing together. In this song, I wanted the lyrics to be ugly and beautiful at the same time, just like I wanted the sound to be ugly and beautiful as well. It starts with pretty chords, then the pre-chorus lyric comes in: ‘You took away a year of my fucking life.’ That’s not exactly subtle. Almost at the minute mark, there’s a really low sine-wave bass that I ran through this MIKE DEAN plug-in that made it sound super distorted. That is representative of what was the most turbulent time in my life, where I was most uncomfortable.

—via Apple Music
